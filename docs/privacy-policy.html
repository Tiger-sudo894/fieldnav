FieldNav Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

FieldNav is a simple outdoor navigation utility. It shows your current GPS coordinates, compass heading, and lets you save locations on your device.

Information FieldNav Uses

FieldNav uses your device location to display latitude, longitude, accuracy, altitude, speed, and heading when available. FieldNav may also use device orientation or motion sensor data to show a compass heading.

Saved Locations

When you save a location, the location name, optional note, coordinates, and related GPS details are stored locally on your device. FieldNav does not upload saved locations to a server.

Data Collection and Sharing

FieldNav does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data to the developer or to third parties. The app does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or account login services.

Permissions

FieldNav asks for location permission so it can show your current position and save field points. The app may ask for motion or orientation access where required by the operating system so the compass can work.

Data Deletion

Saved locations remain on your device until you delete them in the app or remove the app from your device.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/Tiger-sudo894/fieldnav/issues.
